Planet Potluck

This is our little project to promote the sharing of international recipes for potluck events. We know how stressful it can be to come up with exciting food to share at a party so we have set this up to allow you to submit and view recipes from all around the world. We encourage you to send in something authentic from a cuisine you know that is suitable for mass-production. Other users can vote up or down each recipe.

We allow you to sort through recipes by country of origin, by top rated overall and also by dietary requirement.

Please fill in the form stating the country, ingredients, steps and tick whether this recipe fulfils the predetermined dietary requirements. Photos are encouraged!
